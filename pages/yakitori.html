Yakitori

Yakitori is a popular Japanese dish made of grilled chicken skewers. It is seasoned with salt or a savory sauce and cooked over charcoal.

Ingredients:

  • 400 g chicken thigh
  • 2–3 green onions (scallions)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp sake
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1–2 tbsp tare sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken and green onions into small pieces.
  2. Put them onto skewers.
  3. Mix soy sauce, mirin, sake, sugar, ginger, and salt.
  4. Cook the skewers in a pan or on a grill.
  5. Brush with sauce while cooking.
  6. Serve hot.
