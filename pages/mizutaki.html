Mizutaki

Mizutaki is a traditional Japanese hot pot dish made with chicken and vegetables. It is light, healthy, and often enjoyed with family or friends.

Ingredients:

  • 500 g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 liter water
  • 1 napa cabbage
  • 1 carrot
  • 100 g mushrooms
  • 1 block tofu
  • 2 green onions
  • Salt
  • Soy sauce (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil in a large pot.
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until tender.
  3. Add vegetables, tofu, and mushrooms.
  4. Simmer for 10–15 minutes until everything is cooked.
  5. Season lightly with salt.
  6. Serve hot with soy sauce on the side.
