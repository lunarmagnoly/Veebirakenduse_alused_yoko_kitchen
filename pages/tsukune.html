Tsukune

Tsukune are Japanese chicken meatballs, usually served on skewers. They are tender, juicy, and often glazed with a sweet soy sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 400 g ground chicken
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp sake
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 green onions
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix ground chicken, egg, garlic, ginger, and chopped green onions in a bowl.
  2. Add soy sauce, mirin, sake, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Shape the mixture into small meatballs or onto skewers.
  4. Cook in a pan or on a grill until fully cooked.
  5. Brush with sauce while cooking to create a glaze.
  6. Serve hot.
